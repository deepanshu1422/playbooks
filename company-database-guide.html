Phase 1: Data Collection (Week 1)


Method 1: LinkedIn Company Search


Step 1: Find Companies with Indian Employees


  1. Go to LinkedIn
  2. Search: `"Software Engineer" AND location:"Amsterdam" AND "India"`
  3. Filter by: Company size (50-1000+ employees)

          Phase 2: Verification (Week 2)


          Step 1: Check Company Careers Page


          For each company:

          1. Visit `company.com/careers`
          2. Look for:

            3. - "We sponsor visas" language

            - Job postings with "relocation support"

            - International hiring mention


            Step 2: LinkedIn Deep Dive


            1. Go to company LinkedIn page
            2. Click "People" tab
            3. Filter by:

              4. - Location: Current country + "India" (to see who moved from India)

              - Search "visa" in employee profiles (some mention HSM visa)


              1. Count:

                2. - Total Indians at company

                - Indians who moved from India (check their location history)

                - Recent joiners (last 6 months)


                Why this matters:

                • High Indian count = they actively hire Indians
                • Recent joiners = they're hiring NOW

                Step 3: Glassdoor Reviews


                1. Search company on Glassdoor
                2. Filter reviews by:

                  3. - Country: Netherlands/Spain/UAE

                  - Search keyword: "visa"


                  1. Look for:

                    2. - Visa process reviews

                    - International employee experiences

                    - Complaints about visa (= they do sponsor, just slow)
