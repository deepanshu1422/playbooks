üîë KEY PRINCIPLES

1. Document Everything

Every process, every decision, every workflow should be documented. If it's in your head, it's a single point of failure.

2. Systematize Before You Scale

Don't hire more people to fix broken systems. Fix the system, then scale it with people.

3. Measure What Matters

Track inputs (activities) and outputs (results). What gets measured gets improved.

4. Build for Delegation

Every SOP should be written so someone else can execute it without you. Your goal is to work ON the business, not IN it.

5. Quality Over Quantity

Better to have 50 highly successful students than 500 mediocre ones. Reputation is your moat.

6. Automate Ruthlessly

If it can be automated, automate it. If it can't, templatize it. Save human energy for high-value work.

7. Customer Success = Business Success

Your customers' results are your marketing. Obsess over their wins, and growth will follow.

üìä SUCCESS METRICS BY PHASE

Phase 1: 0-$10K/month (Months 1-3)

  • Revenue: $5K-10K/month
  • Customers: 5-10 active clients
  • Email List: 500-1,000 subscribers
  • Content: 10-20 YouTube videos
  • Webinars: 2x/month, 15-25% attendance
  • Team: Just you (maybe VA)

Phase 2: $10K-$50K/month (Months 3-6)

  • Revenue: $25K-50K/month
  • Customers: 25-50 active clients
  • Email List: 2,000-5,000 subscribers
  • Content: 30-50 YouTube videos
  • Webinars: 2-4x/month, 20-30% attendance
  • Team: Founder + VA + Editor

Phase 3: $50K-$100K/month (Months 6-12)

  • Revenue: $75K-100K/month
  • Customers: 75-100 active clients
  • Email List: 8,000-15,000 subscribers
  • Content: 75-100+ YouTube videos
  • Webinars: Weekly, 25-35% attendance
  • Team: 4-6 people

Phase 4: $100K-$250K/month (Year 2)

  • Revenue: $150K-250K/month
  • Customers: 150-250 active clients
  • Email List: 25,000-50,000 subscribers
  • Paid Ads: $10K-30K/month spend
  • Webinars: 2-3x/week, automated + live
  • Team: 10-15 people

Phase 5: $250K+/month (Year 3+)

  • Revenue: $250K-500K+/month
  • Customers: 250-500+ active clients
  • Multiple Products: Starter, Core, VIP tiers
  • Enterprise/B2B: Corporate partnerships
  • Team: 20-50+ people
  • Exit Potential: 3-5x annual revenue multiple
