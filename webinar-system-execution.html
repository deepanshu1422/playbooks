Day 31: LIVE WEBINAR DAY üé§

Deliverable: Your first live webinar delivered

  • Start on time (don't wait for latecomers more than 2-3 min)
  • Record the session (you'll use this for replay)
  • Engage chat every 5-10 minutes ("Type YES if you're following")
  • Stick to your script but be natural
  • When pitching:
    • State the price clearly
    • Explain the payment options (1-pay or payment plan)
    • Give a deadline (48-hour cart close)
    • Address top 3 objections proactively
    • Open Q&A for 15-20 minutes
  • Drop payment link in chat 3 times: during pitch, after Q&A, at the end

üí° Webinar Day Tips

  • Expect 30-50% show-up rate (50 registered = 15-25 live attendees)
  • Don't panic if people drop off midway (normal)
  • Your goal: 1-3 sales from 20 attendees (5-15% conversion)
  • If you get zero sales, that's okay - you'll learn what to fix

Day 32-33: Post-Webinar Follow-Up

Deliverable: Follow-up emails + sales calls booked

  • üìñ Reference: Email Sequences Playbook (post-webinar sequence)
  • Send 3 emails over 48 hours:
    1. Day 1 (6 hours after): "Here's the replay + special offer expires in 48h"
    2. Day 2 (24 hours after): Case study email + "24 hours left"
    3. Day 3 (42 hours after): "Final call - cart closes at midnight"
  • Offer a 15-min "strategy call" to anyone on the fence (book via Calendly)
  • Use these calls to close manually (see Sales Playbook for scripts)

üí° Quick Win

Personally message everyone who stayed till the end: "Hey [name], thanks for attending! Do you have any questions about [program]?" This simple DM can close 1-2 extra sales.

Day 34-35: Webinar Debrief + Iteration

Deliverable: Notes on what worked and what to fix

  • Watch your webinar recording (yes, all of it)
  • Note:
    • Where people dropped off (check Zoom analytics)
    • Which parts felt awkward or too long
    • Which objections came up in Q&A
    • If your pitch was clear and compelling
  • Make quick fixes for next webinar (don't overhaul everything)
  • If you made sales: what convinced them? Ask in a survey.
  • If you made zero sales: was it offer clarity, price, or trust?

‚úã Post-Webinar Evaluation

  • Registration ‚Üí show-up rate (target: 30-50%)
  • Show-up ‚Üí stayed till pitch (target: 40-60%)
  • Pitch ‚Üí sales (target: 10-15% of attendees)
  • Total revenue from webinar (track every dollar)
