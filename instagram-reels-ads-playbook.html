üéØ WHY THE OLD SCRIPTS SUCKED


Problem 1: No Open Loop

  • Old: "I spent ‚Çπ10 Cr on ads. Here's what I learned:"
  • **Why it sucks:** No reason to keep watching. Just tell me the lesson in comments.

Problem 2: No Emotion

  • Old: "We generated ‚Çπ5 Cr in 6 months. The strategy:"
  • **Why it sucks:** Numbers without story = boring. Where's the struggle? The breakthrough moment?

Problem 3: No Pattern Interrupt

  • Old: "Your ads aren't working because of THIS:"
  • **Why it sucks:** Predictable. Viewer knows what's coming. No surprise.

Problem 4: Too Much Teaching, Zero Entertainment

  • Old: "Change this Facebook ad setting..."
  • **Why it sucks:** This is YouTube tutorial energy, not Reels energy.

üî• THE NEW FORMULA: ENTERTAINMENT-FIRST STORYTELLING


Rule 1: Open Loop in First 1 Second

Don't reveal the answer. Create unbearable curiosity.


‚ùå Bad: "Here's how to reduce CAC:"

‚úÖ Good: "I accidentally discovered this and our CAC dropped 80%..."


Why it works: Brain NEEDS to know "what was the accident?"


---


Rule 2: Pattern Interrupts Every 3-5 Seconds

Change something. Constantly.


Visual interrupts:

  • Cut to different angle
  • Zoom in/out suddenly
  • Text pop-in with sound effect
  • Show something unexpected (dashboard, meme, reaction face)

Audio interrupts:

  • Pause mid-sentence
  • Change tone (whisper ‚Üí loud)
  • Add sound effects (cash register, explosion)

Content interrupts:

  • Start story ‚Üí tangent ‚Üí back to story
  • "But wait, it gets worse..."
  • "Plot twist:"

---


Rule 3: Emotional Rollercoaster

Make them FEEL something every 5 seconds.


Emotions that work:

  • **Anger** ("This consultant charged them ‚Çπ5L and did NOTHING")
  • **Surprise** ("Then the client showed me their REAL numbers...")
  • **Hope** ("If this worked for them, it'll work for you")
  • **Fear** ("You're making the same mistake that bankrupted 3 businesses I know")
  • **Schadenfreude** ("Watching a ‚Çπ50L ad campaign crash and burn in real-time")

---


Rule 4: Story Structure (NOT Teaching Structure)


‚ùå Teaching: Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí CTA

‚úÖ Story: Setup ‚Üí Conflict ‚Üí Escalation ‚Üí Twist ‚Üí Resolution


Example:

  • Setup: "Client came to me, spending ‚Çπ10L/month on ads"
  • Conflict: "Zero sales. Absolutely zero."
  • Escalation: "Worse: They'd been doing this for 8 months"
  • Twist: "Turns out, their ads were working perfectly. The problem was..."
  • Resolution: "Fixed one thing. ‚Çπ2.8 Cr in 6 months."

üéØ ADVANCED PATTERN INTERRUPTS


Visual Interrupts (Use Every 4-5 Seconds):


1. **The Zoom Punch**

- Normal framing ‚Üí sudden zoom to face

- Use when revealing something important


2. **The B-Roll Flash**

- Talking ‚Üí cut to dashboard/screenshot for 1 second ‚Üí back to talking

- Keeps visual variety


3. **The Meme Insert**

- Mid-sentence, flash a relevant meme (0.5 seconds)

- Example: "They wanted a refund" ‚Üí *surprised Pikachu face* ‚Üí back


4. **The Text Explosion**

- Big numbers appear ON SCREEN with sound effect

- "‚Çπ3.2 CRORE" *BOOM sound*


5. **The Camera Flip**

- Talking ‚Üí flip to rear camera showing something ‚Üí flip back

- "Let me show you..." ‚Üí *flip to computer screen*


---


Audio Interrupts:


1. **The Whisper-Shout**

- Start sentence whispering ‚Üí END LOUD

- "I'm about to tell you something... THAT CHANGES EVERYTHING"


2. **The Record Scratch**

- Mid-story: *record scratch sound* "Wait. Back up."


3. **The Echo**

- Emphasize key word with echo effect

- "The problem problem problem was..."


4. **The Silence**

- Pause for 1-2 seconds (eternity on Reels)

- Creates discomfort, forces attention


---


Emotional Interrupts:


1. **The Mood Swing**

- Happy ‚Üí suddenly serious

- "So I got the client, great right? No. Because..."


2. **The Self-Deprecation**

- Confident ‚Üí laugh at yourself

- "I'm an expert. Which is why I made this STUPID mistake..."


3. **The Anger Flash**

- Calm ‚Üí sudden frustration

- "And then they told me- UGH I can't even-"
